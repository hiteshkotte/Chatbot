About the Chatbot

Welcome to the BiWi AI Tutor, an intelligent chatbot designed to enhance learning experiences in higher education. Built with cutting-edge technologies, the chatbot provides personalized support to students in the field of educational sciences. Whether you need answers to course-related questions or guidance for self-directed learning, BiWi AI Tutor is here to help.

What Makes It Special?

The BiWi AI Tutor goes beyond simple question-answering. It combines advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) with Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to deliver accurate, context-aware responses. Think of it as your virtual mentor, ready to provide expert advice and learning support anytime, anywhere.

How It Works

The chatbot operates through a robust and intelligent workflow:

  • Understanding Your Query: The chatbot breaks down your question and determines the context, ensuring it fully understands what you need.
  • Finding the Right Information: It retrieves relevant content from course materials such as lecture slides, seminar notes, and organizational documents.
  • Providing Accurate Answers: Using advanced AI techniques, it generates clear, concise, and helpful responses tailored to your query.

Key Features

  • Personalized Support: Receive answers and guidance based on your specific needs.
  • 24/7 Availability: Access learning support anytime, anywhere.
  • Scalable Mentorship: Offers feedback and learning assistance for large student groups.
  • Secure and Transparent: Protects your privacy while providing explainable answers.

Who Can Use It?

The BiWi AI Tutor is ideal for students looking to:

  • Understand complex topics from their courses.
  • Get quick answers to organizational questions like deadlines or schedules.
  • Receive feedback on self-directed learning activities.

Performance You Can Trust

The BiWi AI Tutor has been rigorously evaluated to ensure high-quality performance:

  • Achieved an 87% correctness rate in automated evaluations using GPT-4.
  • Validated through human evaluations by domain experts for reliability and accuracy.

Secure and Reliable

Your data privacy is a top priority. All interactions with the BiWi AI Tutor are processed securely, and sensitive information is safeguarded. The chatbot is designed to provide transparent answers, so you can trust its guidance.
